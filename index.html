What is Shapies?

Kids are engaged in a series of activities where they have to hunt the Shapies, digital creatures populating the world. The Shapies are a little shy. They hide when strangers looks at them. Kids have to become friends with them by learning their language and building their own Shapies avatars to interact with them.

Education Kit

The game is designed for primary school students and their teachers. This game allows you to bring children closer to the world of computer science.

Physical Cards

The cards allow you to build your own custom creatures and make them move around the world.

Augmented Reality App

Augmented reality connects the virtual world with the real world. Interact and share your experience with your friends and find all the hidden creatures.

Digital Creatures

The creatures have a simple but highly customizable appearance. We wanted to give space to everyone's creativity.

Shapies include a bounch of playful activities!

The game is designed to express the creativity of each individual, but also to teach the basics of computer science. Through simple exercises you can discover many topics related to computers and train your skills in computational thinking.
